Concept

This short animation is created to introduce the Reflections and Expositions course at RMIT University. This is an aiding course which helps students to develop graphic and presentation skills for their honours project. This animation uses a storytelling approach to show the content of the course through the journey of an empty box. Students would acquire different skills along the journey, and by the end of the semester, they will be ready to overflow and showcase their amazing works to the world.
