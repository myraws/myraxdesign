Concept

This is a design proposal layout for Bravo Art Jam Gallery in Shenzhen, China. The design brief is to create a colourful and lively vibe that matches with the style of products the gallery wishes to exhibit. It is also required to present the bilingual descriptions in a clear and professional manner, as to cater to a diverse background group of buyers. The layout is designed to have a clean and simple look to allow easy navigation through the contents.
